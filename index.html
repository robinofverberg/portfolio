Life Experience

In the summer of 2023, I worked as a Central-medarbetare at Uppsala Vatten. My responsibilities included providing excellent customer service, conducting regular container inventory checks, maintaining cleanliness at the workplace, and handling the opening and closing procedures. I also assisted in guiding customers when needed. During this role, I honed my customer service skills and learned the importance of efficient inventory management and workplace organization.

Prior to that, I worked at Bildemo as a Däckmontör and Lagermedarbetare during the seasonal periods of October to December 2022 and March to May 2023. In this position, I focused on delivering exceptional customer service, particularly in assisting with wheel assembly in the service bay. I was responsible for ongoing inventory checks to accommodate bookings and walk-in customers, as well as transferring tires to the tire storage area. I also operated a pallet jack for tire transport, sorted tires at the tire storage using a tire lift, and managed tire washing and handling. This experience provided me with valuable hands-on skills in the automotive field.

From November 2021 to April 2022, I worked as a Restaurangbiträde at Sibylla. My role involved providing excellent customer service, operating the cash register system, managing sales, conducting regular inventory checks, and preparing the restaurant for the next day's operations. This position allowed me to further develop my customer service and organizational skills in a fast-paced environment.

Additionally, in February 2019, I gained experience as a Butiksmedarbetare at Coop Nära during a work experience program (PRAO). My responsibilities included ensuring the store was well-maintained by restocking and organizing products, as well as providing excellent customer service.

In September and October 2017, I worked as a Butiksmedarbetare at Lelles MC, where I focused on packing e-commerce orders, restocking products, maintaining store cleanliness, and providing customer service. These early experiences helped me build a strong foundation in retail operations and customer service.
